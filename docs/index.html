Firmware Flashing Tool

Use this page to flash a new Aeroq PCB, recover your device, or install different firmware over USB. For routine updates, use the device’s local web UI or Home Assistant.

1. Connect your device to your computer via USB.
2. Click one of the actions below.
3. Keep this window open until flashing completes.
