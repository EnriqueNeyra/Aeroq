Factory Flash & Recovery

Use this page to flash a brand-new PCB or recover a device over USB. For routine updates, use the device’s local web UI or Home Assistant.

1. Connect your Aeroq to your computer via USB.
2. Click one of the actions below.
3. Keep this window open until flashing completes.

Installs the latest Aeroq firmware, bootloader & partition layout.

Manual .bin (advanced)
Flash a specific firmware binary from your computer. Use if support gave you a test build or you need to pin a version.
